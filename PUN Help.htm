Pokémon Universe Network Help File

Press the TAB key to interact with NPCs and other players.

Press the + key to increase volume, the - key to decrease it, or the M key to mute it.

If you cannot move, press the N key, or type /stuck in the chat box.

Please note that both the arrow keys and the WSAD keys may be used to run.

If a bike is obtained, only costumes programmed with a proper bike sprite may use the bike.

To use the running shoes, press and hold the B key while walking.

By pressing the X key and then pressing an arrow key or one of the WSAD keys, your direction will change but you will not move. (This can be used to talk to your walking Pokémon)

On certain holidays, or months of holidays in the cases of Halloween and Christmas, an in-game event may arise based on the holiday. (Based on EST in the US & Canada.)
